Courses

Learn more about our academy and start learning today with our courses made especially for you.

We offer blended learning solutions that help people reach their full potential. Our unique online adaptive learning platform has a proven track-record in creating an effective learning path customised to the aptitude of each student.

Our blended learning concept helps to create a classroom experience where you can skip basic knowledge transfer and dive right into cases and real-life examples. Trainers have clear insights in the activity of their students. This approach has proven to increase classroom effectiveness while at the same time sparking enthusiasm in students.

We offer both online courses as well as fully integrated blended courses where we provide (in-house) training as well. We can help you by designing courses accustomed to your needs.

Finally we have recently started offering curated third-party content through partnerships with trainers and educators.

Online training

Our online courses are offered in our unique adaptive learning environment which we fully developed in-house.

Our learning module features a wide range of content options including animations, downloadable text and on-screen. Students choose what suits them best.

Our adaptive engine features content on multiple difficulty levels that create a fully customised learning path for each student. Students have full insight in the level they achieve. In the case of Company training, trainers and/or HR have access to student progress.

We offer both stand-alone online courses as well as fully integrated blended courses where you can add (in-house) classroom training as well. We can help you by designing courses accustomed to your needs.

Corporate and inhouse training

Our blended learning solutions can help your company’s employees reach their full potential. We combine the best of our in-house developed online adaptive learning module with effective classroom training.

Our unique online adaptive learning platform has a proven track-record in creating an effective learning path customised to the aptitude of each student. We use this platform to prepare students for classroom training. Trainers have clear insights in the activity of their students which allows them to customise the classroom experience based on the individual student needs.

Trainers can now create a classroom experiences where they can skip basic knowledge transfer and dive right into cases and real-life examples. This approach results in an exponential improvement in classroom effectiveness while at the same time sparking enthusiasm in students.

We have a wide network of trainers which includes seasoned professionals as well as University professors which operate at the top of their field.
