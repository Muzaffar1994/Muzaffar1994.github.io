Corporate strategy development

We can help you identify opportunities for improving the value of your business.

Our advanced value analytics framework aims to identify key areas where you can add value. It helps you to optimise capital- and resource allocation and enables you to accelerate growth and return on capital.

We combine corporate strategy development with our extensive M&A experience to design an optimal integrated strategy aimed at optimising your portfolio.

Finally we believe in a hands-on approach that enables decisive execution of your strategy. We therefore translate your corporate strategy to a business unit strategy with clear executable steps towards value creation.

Business unit strategy development

We have a proven track record in assisting companies with developing their business unit strategy. We apply a rigorous data-driven approach that allows for clear focus on the areas that matter.

We will work in close co-operation with your team to capture the full benefits of your organisation’s knowledge and capabilities while at the same time ensuring commitment and buy-in of your key stakeholders. This approach has proven to be effective both in terms of strategy development as well as execution.

Finally, we aim to enable continuous organisational learning by clearly communicating and internalising the key drivers for the success of your strategy. This final step moves away from a rigid strategy and rather helps you to adapt and evolve where and when required.

Business analysis

Our business analysis capabilities support your strategic decision making by translating your strategy to financial impact. We apply a scenario approach to identify those scenarios with the highest reward to risk ratio.

We further help you identify opportunities for improving the value of your business. Our value analytics framework aims to identify your key value drivers and allows you to introduce value steering in your company.

We further have extensive experience in developing business cases for large investment projects and/or business cases for review of strategic options.

Based on our corporate- as well as consulting experience, we are able to provide you with advise that allows you to execute.
