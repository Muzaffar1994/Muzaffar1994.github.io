15/16 Group partners as full service ops support with CPG programs & manufacturers looking to properly execute, streamline and accelerate their operation.

With over 30 years of ground floor leadership experience, we have led start-ups programs, family-owned businesses and those managed by F&B venture/private equity groups ranging from pre-rev to $100MM+ in sales. Our product experiences include everything from fresh to frozen, retail to food service, raw to ready to eat, dry mixes, beverages, prepared meals, various snack programs, direct to consumer, dairy products, pet food, and plant based.
